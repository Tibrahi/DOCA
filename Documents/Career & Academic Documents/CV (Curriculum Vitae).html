Universal ATS-Optimized CV Guide

Designed to pass AI screening systems (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Taleo)

Applicable for: Business, Finance, Healthcare, Engineering, Tech, Marketing, Entry-level, and more.

Universal CV Structure (AI-Safe Order)

Use this exact order for maximum compatibility:

  1. Header
  2. Professional Summary
  3. Core Competencies / Skills
  4. Work Experience
  5. Education
  6. Certifications / Training
  7. Additional Information (Optional)

What To Include

Header Details

  • Full Name (bold, largest text)
  • Professional Title (match job role)
  • Phone (international format)
  • Professional email
  • LinkedIn (optional but recommended)
  • City, Country

Professional Summary Formula

[Job Title] + [Years of Exp] + [Core Strengths] + [Value] + [Measurable Achievement]

Core Competencies (Keywords)

Use keywords exactly as written in the job description.

Technical

  • Microsoft Excel
  • CRM Systems
  • Data Entry
  • Inventory Management

Professional

  • Communication
  • Team Collaboration
  • Time Management
  • Problem Solving

Work Experience (Critical)

Format: Job Title | Company | Month Year – Month Year

  • Start bullets with Strong Action Verbs (Managed, Led, Increased, Analyzed).
  • Show Measurable Results (Reduced processing time by 30%).
  • Focus on impact, not duties.

Education & Certifications

  • Degree / Certification Name
  • Institution / Issuing Body
  • Year
  • Optional: GPA (if strong), Relevant coursework

Do NOT Include / Rejection Triggers

Header Exclusions

  • Photo (unless legally required)
  • Age / Date of birth
  • Religion
  • Marital status
  • National ID
  • Gender
  • Full home address

What Causes AI Rejection

  • Missing keywords
  • Over-designed layout
  • No measurable results
  • Job titles unclear
  • Dates missing
  • Keyword stuffing
  • Unreadable fonts
  • Experience not aligned with job

Optional Sections Warning

Only include Languages, Volunteer Experience, or Awards if relevant. Do not add irrelevant sections just to fill space.

Page Layout Rules

Structure Constraints

  • ✔ Single column
  • ✔ Left aligned
  • ✖ No tables
  • ✖ No text boxes
  • ✖ No graphics or icons
  • ✖ No headers/footers with important info

Margins & Length

Setting Value
Margins 0.5 – 1 inch
Students / Entry 1 page
Mid-level 1–2 pages
Senior Max 2 pages (rarely 3)

Formatting & Final Checklist

Typography & Files

Preferred Fonts:

  • Calibri
  • Arial
  • Helvetica
  • Times New Roman

Font Sizes:

  • Name: 16–18pt
  • Headings: 12–14pt
  • Body: 10–11pt

File Type:

Best: .docx
Second: Clean exported PDF
Avoid: Scanned PDF, Canva complex layouts.

Final Universal Checklist

  • ✔ Job title matches job posting
  • ✔ Keywords aligned with description
  • ✔ Clear employment dates
  • ✔ Measurable results included
  • ✔ No personal sensitive data
  • ✔ No grammar errors
  • ✔ Saved as .docx
  • ✔ Filename: FirstName_LastName_CV_2026.docx
